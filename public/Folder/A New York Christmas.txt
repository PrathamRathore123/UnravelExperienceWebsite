Day 2 — Lights, Ice & Broadway
Breakfast at Lotte. Then a private walking tour through Central Park, Gapstow Bridge, and the Upper East Side. Every corner looks like a movie because it probably was one.
Spend the afternoon on Fifth Avenue. Legendary window displays. Christmas markets. The energy of the city at full volume.
Later, ice skating at Rockefeller Center or Bryant Park. Then Broadway. Choose from & Juliet, The Lion King, or Moulin Rouge!
The kind of night you'll talk about for years.
Included:
●	Breakfast at Lotte New York Palace
●	Private walking tour of Central Park, Gapstow Bridge, and Upper East Side (iconic movie landmarks)
●	Free afternoon to explore Fifth Avenue window displays and Christmas markets
Not Included (Can Be Added Upon Request):

Day 3 — Aerial Magic & City Lights
Breakfast in bed or brunch at Balthazar in SoHo. Then MoMA or The Edge for morning skyline views.
Free afternoon. Shop through SoHo, Nolita, or the West Village. Café-hop. Get lost in the best way.
Then the highlight: a doors-off helicopter flight over New York City. Empire State Building. One World Trade. Statue of Liberty. Adrenaline. Awe. The whole thing.
Included:
●	Doors-off helicopter flight over New York City
●	Roundtrip luxury transfers to/from helicopter experience
Not Included:
●	Breakfast/brunch at Balthazar (arrange on your own)
●	MoMA or The Edge entry tickets (arrange on your own)
●	Afternoon shopping and café-hopping (explore independently)

Day 4 — Christmas Day at Aman New York
Slow breakfast at Lotte Palace. Optional Christmas mass at St. Patrick's Cathedral, right across the street.
Spend the afternoon however you want. Bryant Park Winter Village. Hot cocoa. A horse carriage ride through Central Park. Summit One Vanderbilt for that interactive art installation everyone's talking about.
In the evening, an exclusive festive dinner at Aman New York. Private table. Candle-lit. A Christmas menu crafted just for this night.
Return to Lotte Palace for your final night. Maybe a quiet toast overlooking the city lights.
Included:
●	Breakfast at Lotte New York Palace
●	Exclusive festive dinner at Aman New York (December 25 only, private table, limited-time Christmas menu)

Not Included:

Breakfast at leisure. One last look at the skyline.

Leave with a heart full of Christmas magic and a camera roll straight out of a movie.

10. Packing & Weather
New York Weather in December:
●	Temperature: -1°C–7°C (30°F–45°F)
●	Conditions: Cold, occasional snow or rain, windy
●	Daylight hours are short (sunrise around 7:15 AM, sunset around 4:30 PM)
What to Pack:
●	Warm layers (sweaters, thermal underlayers)
●	Heavy winter coat
●	Comfortable walking shoes (waterproof, warm)
●	Scarf, gloves, hat
●	Umbrella
●	Dressier outfits for evening experiences (Broadway, Aman dinner)
●	Phone charger, adapter (USA uses Type A and B plugs)
Pro Tip: New York in December is cold but magical. Dress warmly and in layers so you can enjoy outdoor experiences like ice skating and holiday lights without freezing.
