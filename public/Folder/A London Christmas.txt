2. Day-by-Day Breakdown
Day 1 — Arrival & Royal Beginnings
Welcome to London.
Your private premium transfer meets you at the airport and drives you to your hotel. Check in and settle into your room.
At 2:00 PM, your private guide meets you for a 2-hour walking tour through London's royal history. Start with a leisurely stroll around St James — discover its origins, hear about duels fought and fortunes made, and see where the aristocracy partied. Visit St James Palace, the London home of Henry VIII and where King Charles was proclaimed King. Walk across the Mall and see Buckingham Palace framed by St James Park's formal gardens. Pass the Duke of Wellington mansion, then hop on a London bus to Knightsbridge.
Once inside Harrods, explore the magnificent food hall before being welcomed into the exclusive Moët et Chandon Bar. Enjoy a crisp glass of Impérial Brut champagne with chef-created sweet or savory canapés.
Included:
●	Private premium airport transfer
●	One-way transfer from hotel to tour starting point
●	Private royal history walking tour (2 hours, starting 2:00 PM)
●	Private guide
●	Glass of Moët Impérial Brut champagne at Harrods
●	Sweet or savory canapés pairing
Tour Duration: 2 hours
 Meeting Time: 2:00 PM

Day 2 — A British Christmas
The morning is yours. Covent Garden. A quiet café. Your call.
At 6:00 PM, your guide meets you for a private 3-hour Christmas lights walking tour. Get in the holiday spirit as you see London's spectacular Christmas lights, decorations, markets, and window displays. Discover Covent Garden's festive windows, learn where to admire the best lights, and explore Regent Street's glowing arches and Carnaby Street's displays. Enjoy shopping opportunities along the way.
Hot chocolate, mulled wine, or a soft drink included as you stroll through the hidden corners only locals know.
Included:
●	One-way transfer from hotel to tour meeting point
●	Private Christmas lights walking tour (3 hours, starting 6:00 PM)
●	Private guide
●	Glass of hot chocolate, mulled wine, or soft drink
Tour Duration: 3 hours
 Meeting Time: 6:00 PM

Day 3 — The Markets Trail
Today, you're chasing London's markets.
London is renowned for its street markets — gourmet food, antiques, cutting-edge fashion — but knowing where to find what can be a challenge. Your private guide and driver take you to some of London's most celebrated markets. Stop by Portobello Market in Notting Hill, taste delicious foods at Borough Market, explore the vibrant Camden Markets, and stroll around Covent Garden and Old Spitalfields.
Receive insider advice on where to shop and what to buy. Travel around London's neighborhoods in your private vehicle. Explore at your own pace.
Included:
●	Private guide & driver for 8 hours
●	Market tastings
●	Customizable tour based on your preferences
Tour Duration: 8 hours (from start time)

9. Packing & Weather
London Weather in December:
●	Temperature: 4°C–8°C (39°F–46°F)
●	Conditions: Cold, often damp, occasional rain or drizzle
●	Daylight hours are short (sunrise around 8 AM, sunset around 4 PM)
What to Pack:
●	Warm layers (sweaters, thermal underlayers)
●	Waterproof jacket or coat
●	Comfortable walking shoes (waterproof preferred)
●	Scarf, gloves, hat
●	Umbrella
●	Dressier outfits for evening experiences (West End, dinners)
●	Phone charger, adapter (UK uses Type G plugs)
Pro Tip: London in December is magical, but it's cold. Dress in layers so you can adjust as you move between outdoor markets and cozy indoor spots.
